Neuro Linguistic Programmnig

WHAT IS IT?

The process in which to understand how you organize your thinking, feeling, how you use the language and your behavior to produce the results you want. In essence (Neuro) Your mind, your nervous system and how you think, (Linguistic) the language you use to communicate, (Programming) how you order your behaviors and actions to generate results

HOW DOES NLP HELP

NLP provides a skill for creating change. If you want to have more choices about your behavior, emotions and develop new abilities in your thinking NLP can help. NLP is to help you master your life and you achieve this by controlling your mind. It allows you to notice patterns about how you communicate with yourself and the others. It unravels how you process information through the 5 senses (pictures, sounds, feelings, smells, tastes) and how you translate and interpret thses information. It’s the strategy behind your thinking and behavior which makes the difference when generating successful outcomes.
